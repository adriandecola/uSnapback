uSnapback

This tool will guide you through a short, four-step workflow to gather everything needed for snapback primer design:

  1. Amplicon Sequence – the full region to be amplified (including forward primer and the complement of the reverse primer).
  2. Primer Details – forward and reverse primer lengths.
  3. Variant Nucleotide – the single-base variant (SNV) location and type.
  4. Target Tm - the desired snapback melting temperature for the wild-type sequence.

After collecting this information, uSnapback will calculate a snapback design that meets your specified wild-type Tm that maximazes the metling temperature difference between the wild and variant alleles.
